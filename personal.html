My Tech Genesis

In the humble days of 2004, in Barretos/SP Brazil, my tech saga kicked off. It all began at ITERP, where I immersed myself in the enigmatic world of hardware and computer upkeep. At the tender age of 14, I was already dissecting PCs at home, troubleshooting motherboards, power supplies – you name it. The family dubbed me the tech prodigy; I was the go-to guru when things went tech haywire. Picture this: a dial-up dance with the internet at a sluggish 250 kbps, patiently waiting until midnight to catch that sweet one-pulse phone rate. Navigating the vastness of the dial-up internet, cruising on BRASNET with mIRC, using scripts, aliases, and sliding into those ICQ or MSN DMs like a digital smooth operator.

Broadband Revolution

We upgraded from the "lite" Speedy, cruising at 250 kbps, to the "pro" plan, a whopping 2 Mbps. Despite countless calls to the operator, no tech magic could boost my speed. Fast forward to today, in my own fortress, celebrating the fiber optic era with a blazing 1 Gbps up and down. Farewell, endless waits!

Academic Expedition

The academic expedition was a wild ride, from Cetec to the Justice Federal Court in Barretos. I dabbled in law, but the real lift-off happened with the Systems Information degree at UniFEB. My thesis? An epic mission, utilizing PMBOK and RUP to navigate the galaxy of software project management. Back in '08, I was coding Grease Monkey scripts to spice up Orkut scraps, making it rain scraps. The passion for code was taking off, playing with Linux distros, Windows, and catching the meme wave.

From Law to Code

Transitioning from legal codes to lines of code was a journey. At Senac, I dove deep into laser printer maintenance, reaching the stratosphere with web design in Adobe CS3. Photoshop, Fireworks, Dreamweaver, Flash – all aboard this tech hyperloop.

Digital Life's Comedy and Cybernetic Chronicles

Recall the days of MSN, Orkut, and those cybernetic NetBIOS hacks? My website was the ultimate hangout on the web, hosting viruses and MIRC scripts. Social networks were blossoming, and the internet was transforming before our neural networks.

Automation Odyssey

With my love for automation, life became a symphony of meticulously programmed routines. Timers, Raspberry Pi, networks with Pihole, and more – tech orchestrates the rhythm of my existence. Predicting the future, from the last gasp of the propane tank to the electric bill, is my constant algorithmic mindset. Meticulous is the orbital path; predict before you lament.

Professional Wake-Up

Ready to convert passion into propulsion, this site isn't just a portfolio; it's a launchpad to explore the journey of someone who orbits the tech universe. The lines of the future code are in front of me, and I'm ready to script each cosmic command. The goal is crystal: capitalizing on development and programming. The future's coded, and I'm ready to propel it forward.
